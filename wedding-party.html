✦ Look & Feel

What to Wear

👡
Shoes
Open-toed nude shoes — flats or heels, whatever you're most comfortable in.
✨
Jewelry
Minimal and simple. Silver or gold both work.
No Apple Watches please 😊
💅
Nails
Neutral or light pink. Nothing bright or loud.
💇
Hair
Everyone will have their hair done professionally. Styles will be either down or half up, half down — your choice on the exact look.
Hair must be clean (no styling products) and dry with just a blowout — NO flat iron — a maximum of 2 days before the event.
💄
Makeup
Everyone except Kailey and Milianci will have their makeup done professionally. Please arrive with a clean face — no makeup. Natural, not heavy or dramatic is the vibe.

✦ From the Artist

Hair & Makeup Reminders

Neidaliz Garcia Artistry
Artist Reminders
  • Arrive without makeup or hair products. Eyebrows must be clean and groomed.
  • Avoid sun exposure and protect your skin with sunscreen in the days leading up to the event.
  • If you have allergies, notify us in advance. Take an allergy pill day-of and bring eye drops if needed.
  • All clients must be ready in the same room to keep the schedule running smoothly.
  • Hair must be clean and completely dry (blow-dried). Wet hair cannot be serviced.
  • If your hair is very curly, blow-dry and straighten it 1–2 days in advance to control frizz.
  • If wearing natural curls, pre-style with your usual products before the service.
  • Wear a button-down shirt or robe to avoid messing up your hair and makeup when changing.
  • Hair extension application has an additional fee of $45 — must be requested in advance.
  • Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time.
  • Punctuality is essential — the artist is not responsible for delays caused by the client.
  • Please arrange a spacious, well-lit area with air conditioning.
  • If you have hair or makeup inspiration, have a photo or video ready for reference.
